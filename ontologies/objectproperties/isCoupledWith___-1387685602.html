Annotations (1)

  • comment "Denotes that two artifacts are coupled with each other. In principle, coupling is a kind of dependency. However, the direction of the dependency relationship is unclear. For example, Artifact A and B could both depend on a business rule, leading to common commits. Because of this, it is not a subproperty of dependsOn." ()
